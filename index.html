About Us

The purpose of Theta Tau is to develop and maintain a high standard of professional interest among its members,
and to unite them in a strong bond of fraternal fellowship.

Our foundation is supported by our three pillars: Brotherhood, Professionalism, and Community Service. As the Rho Delta chapter of Theta Tau,
we aim to grow our community, develop our skills as engineers, and support each other through our own personal adventures as brothers.
Our colony was initially founded on December 10th, 2011, and with a few short years of hard work and dedication we were installed as the
Rho Delta chapter on November 9th, 2013.
