About Us

The purpose of Theta Tau is to develop and maintain a high standard of professional interest among our members, and to unite them in a strong bond of fraternal fellowship. Our foundation is supported by our three pillars: Brotherhood, Professionalism, and Community Service. As the Rho Delta chapter of Theta Tau, we aim to grow our community, develop our skills as engineers, and support each other through our own personal adventures as brothers.Our colony was initially founded on December 10th, 2011, and with a few short years of hard work and dedication we were installed as the Rho Delta chapter on November 9th, 2013.

Our Pillars

Brotherhood

Rho Delta is committed to building life-long bonds between its members. Through outdoor barbeque events, chapter movie nights, and extravagant holiday celebrations Rho Delta strives develop deep connections between its members and promote the core values of the Theta Tau Fraternity.

Professional Development

Rho Delta is constantly looking to improve and build professionalism among its members through many different avenues such as resume and interview improvement workshops, industry tours, and making valuable connections within industry.

Community Service

Rho Delta seeks to better the community in Reno by exposing the youth to exciting, interactive engineering ideas. Rho Delta also does volunteer work to assist the less fortunate in the community by organizing events like Theta Tau Trick-or-Canning, where generous members of the community donate non-perishable food items for a good cause.
